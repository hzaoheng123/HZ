Hao Zheng 郑昊


I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Center for Biomedical Image Computing and Analytics (CBICA) of University of Pennsylvania. I obtained my Ph.D. from University of Notre Dame in 2021, and my B.Sc. from University of Science and Technology of China in 2016. My research focuses on the intersection of medical image analysis and artificial intelligence, especially in designing data-efficient learning methods and improving model generalization on multi-domain/site data. I have a broad interest in image segmentation and classification, geometric learning, and scientific data visualization.

I am on the 2023-2024 job market. Please feel free to reach out if there is a potential match with your institution.
